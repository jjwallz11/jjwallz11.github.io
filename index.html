About Me:

I’m Johnny Paredez III (the third), a passionate software engineer whose journey started far from the keyboard — deep in the White Mountains of Arizona. I grew up building fences and porches with my dad, falling in love with hands-on problem solving long before I touched a line of code. In high school, I cracked open my first PC and started learning C+, and it clicked — technology became my second nature.

After serving in the military and a lot of things later, life took a turn. My fiancée — a loving, kind, wonderful woman — tragically passed away in 2023. It broke me open… but I also knew where to turn. I made the decision to honor her legacy by stepping into software development, not just as a career, but as a calling. I joined App Academy in September 2023 and after a couple of failed attempts at full time and a couple of extenuating circumstances I finally graduated in April 2025 — building full-stack projects, mastering tools like Flask, React, PostgreSQL, and learning how to bring big ideas to life with code.

Today, I build with passion, purpose, and resilience. Whether it’s a SpongeBob-themed restaurant management app or a collaborative finance tracker, I bring both creativity and grit to every project. I love solving real problems, supporting my teammates, and learning new tools like TypeScript and FastAPI along the way. Above all, I code with faith, with heart, and in memory of the one who inspired it all.
